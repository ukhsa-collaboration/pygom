Pre-defined examples - common epi models#

We have defined a set of models common_models, most of them commonly used in epidemiology. They are there as examples and also to save time for users. Most of them are of the compartmental type, and we use standard naming conventions i.e. S = Susceptible, E = Exposed, I = Infectious, R = Recovered.
